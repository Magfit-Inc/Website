What the FAQ?

Do I need a membership to use MagFit?

We never require you to have an up to date gym membership to use the MagFit app. Our goal is to help you find a community worth joining.

Does it cost money for gyms to be on MagFit?

We offer a free plan for gyms to join MagFit. Sign your gym up today!

Does it cost money to download MagFit?

It is free to make a MagFit account. Download for free today!

I own multiple gyms, do I need to onboard them individually?

We encourage gym owners to upload all gyms under their control. Onboard your primary gym first, then once in the dashboard you can add additional gyms to your account.
